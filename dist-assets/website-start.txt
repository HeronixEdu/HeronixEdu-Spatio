SPATIO STUDIO 1.0.0
===================

Free, offline 3D modeling for kids ages 6-14.
From Heronix Education Systems LLC.

Thank you for downloading Spatio Studio. We hope your students
have fun building, painting, and 3D printing what they make.

------------------------------------------------------------
TO RUN THE APP
------------------------------------------------------------

Open the SpatioStudio folder.
Double-click "SpatioStudio.exe".

That's it. The app starts. No install, no login, no internet
needed, nothing to set up. The first launch takes about 30
seconds while the app extracts its Chromium runtime to your
user profile folder. Every launch after that is instant.

You can keep the SpatioStudio folder anywhere you like — your
Desktop, your Documents folder, an external drive, or a network
share. The app runs in place; nothing has to be "installed"
in the traditional sense.

------------------------------------------------------------
WHERE YOUR WORK GOES
------------------------------------------------------------

When you click Save inside the app, your project is saved to a
folder called "Spatio Projects" inside your Documents folder.
The app creates this folder automatically the first time you
save. You can open Documents → Spatio Projects in Windows
Explorer at any time to find your saved files.

You can also Save As to choose any other folder you like — a
USB stick, a OneDrive folder, the desktop, anywhere.
